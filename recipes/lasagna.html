Jimmy's Lasagna

Description

This is Jimmy's recipe. This recipe has been handed down from on high to a long line of Jimmy's.

Without this recipe my Jimmy's would be perpetually rustled. It's for this reason, I always make this lasagna.

Ingredients

  1. 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  2. 1 pound lean ground beef
  3. salt and pepper to taste
  4. 1 (16 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  5. 1 clove garlic, minced
  6. 1/2 pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  7. 1/2 pound shredded Cheddar cheese
  8. 1 pint ricotta cheese
